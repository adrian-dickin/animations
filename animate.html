Reels basic reel of three

The 3 coloured circles represent 3 dancers in a reel.

The red 1 dancer is facing the green 2 dancer, the blue 3 dancer is facing the other two.

When you play the animation notice how

  • the vertical direction alternates, 1 and 3 match while 2 is the opposite
  • they all follow the same path

Press Play to see the path the red dancer takes.

The person at the back of the reel is the one who is looking at the back of the person in front of them.

For the reel to work the person at the back of the reel (blue 3 dancer) must change their speed, if they don't then they will collide with red 1.

Press Play to see the collision.

For the reel to work this person must hold back until the other dancer has passed in front, then they must speed up to reach the top in time.

Press Play to speed change.

The 2nd half of the reel is the opposite of the 1st half. Red 1 and Green 2 are still facing each other with blue 3 facing both of them, but the order is reversed.

Here Blue 3 must hold back again..

Press Play to see the 2nd half of the reel.
